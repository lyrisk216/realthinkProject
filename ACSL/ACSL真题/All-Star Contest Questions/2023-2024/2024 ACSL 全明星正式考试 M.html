PROBLEM: Thirteen is a popular multi-person card game. The game uses multiple decks of playing cards. Each deck of cards contains 52 cards, 2 through Ace in 4 suits (Clubs, Diamonds, Hearts, and Spades). In this game, the suit of the card is ignored and 2s are high. Thus, the rank of the cards, from low to high, is: 3, 4, 5, …, 9, T (ten), J, Q, K, A, 2. 
 
The game is played in rounds and each player’s goal is to discard all of the cards in her hand, subject to the rules below. The game begins with Player #1 discarding, followed by Player #2, Player #3,..., rotating back to Player #1 and so on. Each round has the following structure:
 
● The player starting the round determines the largest X for “X-of-a-kind” that she has of each rank (e.g., 2-of-a-kind, 3-of-a-kind). Of those X-of-a-kind, the starting player discards all of the cards that she has with the lowest rank.
 
● Players after that can discard cards only if they have that exact same number of cards of a rank that is higher than the rank just discarded; otherwise, they pass for the round. The round stops when no one can play that exact number of cards of a higher rank. 
 
The next round is started by the last player to discard. If that player is now out of cards, the next round is started by the player after that one. The game ends when all but one player has discarded all of their cards.
 
You will be given an integer, N, for the number of players, and then N strings each containing the initial deal for each player. The cards in each deal are separated by a single space. N will be between 3 and 10, inclusive. Output the order in which the players discarded the last card in their hand followed by the player who still has cards in her hand. The output is a single string, each player’s number separated by a single space.
 
EXAMPLE:
Input:
3
JC 6C QD AH 8H TS 5D JD 7D 8D 9C 6H QS 9H 4H KH KS
9D 7S JS 2S 8C TC JH 7C TD 4D 6D 4C 5S 3H 2D 3C 6S
QC 2H AC KC 5H TH KD AD AS 4S 3S 9S 8S 5C 2C QH 3D
 
The players’ hands, in sorted order, are shown in the box at the right. Round 1 starts with Player #1. She has no 3-of-a-kind, 4-of-a-kind, etc. The most-of-a-kind that she has is pairs. She has pairs of 6s, 8s, 9s, Jacks, Queens, and Kings. The lowest rank of the pairs is 6, so she discards two 6s. Player #2 must discard a card where he has exactly a pair, and a higher rank than 6. He discards his pair of 7s.
 
Player #3 discards his pair of Queens. Player #1 discards her pair of Kings. Player #2 discards his pair of 2s. The round ends because no player can discard a pair of cards with a higher rank.
 
The table below shows the cards at the start of each round and the discarded cards for the round:
 
 
Output:
3 1 2
 
INPUT: A positive integer N, followed by N strings. Each string contains the cards for each player’s hand at the beginning of the game. The cards in each player’s hand are represented by the value (3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, T, J, Q, K, A, 2) and the suit (C, D, H, S) as 2-character strings, each card separated by a single space. There will be no more than 10 players and no more than 100 cards in each player’s hand.
 
OUTPUT: Output the order in which the players finish the game including the last player who didn’t finish, each separated by a single space. All players except one are guaranteed to finish the game.

问题: Thirteen 是一种流行的多人纸牌游戏。该游戏采用多副纸牌，每副纸牌有 52 张牌，包括四种花色：Clubs（梅花）、Diamonds（方块）、Hearts（红心）和 Spades（黑桃），牌面从 2 到 A。在这个游戏中，牌的花色不作区分，且 2 是最大的牌。因此，所有牌按牌面从小到大排列为：3，4，5，…，9，T ( 10 )，J，Q，K，A，2。
 
游戏按轮次进行。每位玩家的目标是在遵守以下规则的前提下，出完手中所有牌。游戏从 1 号玩家开始，依次是 2 号、3 号玩家 ......。所有玩家依次出牌后又回到 1 号玩家继续出牌。每轮游戏的具体规则如下：
 
● 每轮游戏的首位玩家需确定她手中每种牌面的最大张数 X，写作 “X-of-a-kind”，即由 X 张相同牌面的牌组成。例如，2-of-a-kind (即 2 张相同牌面的牌)、3-of-a-kind (即 3 张相同牌面的牌）。在这些 X-of-a-kind 中，首位玩家打出她手中所有牌面最小的牌。
 
● 之后的玩家只能在拥有相同数量但牌面更大的牌组时才能出牌，否则此轮不能出牌。当没有玩家能够打出相同数量但牌面更大的牌组时，这一轮游戏结束。
 
下一轮从上一轮最后出牌的玩家开始。如果该玩家此时已经没有牌了，那么下一轮就由该玩家后面的玩家开始。若除某一位玩家外，其他所有玩家均已出完手中的牌，则游戏结束。
 
你将得到一个整数 N，代表玩家人数；然后得到 N 个字符串，每个字符串包含一位玩家的初始牌，每张牌之间用一个空格隔开。N 的值介于 3 和 10 之间（包括 3 和 10）。最后以单个字符串的形式，按照玩家出完牌的先后顺序（未出完牌的玩家排在最后）输出玩家编号，每个编号之间用一个空格隔开。
 
示例
输入：
3
JC 6C QD AH 8H TS 5D JD 7D 8D 9C 6H QS 9H 4H KH KS
9D 7S JS 2S 8C TC JH 7C TD 4D 6D 4C 5S 3H 2D 3C 6S
QC 2H AC KC 5H TH KD AD AS 4S 3S 9S 8S 5C 2C QH 3D
 
玩家手中的牌按牌面从小到大排列，记录在右侧方框中。第一轮游戏由 1号玩家开始。她手中牌组最大数量为 2 张，包括牌面为 6, 8, 9, J, Q, K 的牌组。除此之外，玩家手中没有数量为 3 张、4 张或更多张的牌组。所有牌组中最小牌组是牌面为 6 的牌组，所以她打出两张 6。接下来，2 号玩家必须恰好拥有两张牌面比 6 更大的牌，才能出牌。于是 2号玩家打出两张 7。
 
 
 3 号玩家打出两张 Q。1 号玩家打出两张 K。2 号玩家打出两张 2。随后由于没有玩家能打出两张牌面更大的牌，本轮游戏结束。

输入: 一个正整数 N，后面跟着 N 个字符串。每个字符串包含游戏开始时每位玩家手中的牌。其中每张牌用两个字符表示，这两个字符分别为牌面大小（3、4、5、6、7、8、9、T、J、Q、K、A、2）和花色（C、D、H、S），且每张牌之间用一个空格隔开。每轮游戏玩家不超过 10 人，每人手中的牌不超过 100 张。
 
输出: 按照玩家完成游戏的顺序输出玩家编号，包括最后一名未完成游戏的玩家，每位玩家之间用一个空格隔开。除一名玩家外，所有玩家都肯定会完成游戏，即出完手中所有的牌。
